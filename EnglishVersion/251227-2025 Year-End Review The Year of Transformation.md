### **2025 Year-End Review: The Year of Transformation**

---

2025 passed in the blink of an eye. I never used to write year-end reviews, but this year is exceptional. It has been the year of fastest growth and most profound transformation in my 20 years of life—not just in my career, but in my inner core, cognition, and mindset.

In 2025, I realized I am no longer a naive and immature boy, but a man who must stand tall and shoulder his responsibilities.

In 2025, I defined my ultimate goal for the next five years and began putting it into action.

In 2025, I constantly cultivated my inner self. I shed my introversion, realizing I must connect with more outstanding people, strive for opportunities, take responsibility, and communicate effectively. I stopped being anxious about trivialities and truly learned to seek answers from within; my heart has never been so full, nor my inner strength so formidable.

In 2025, my life was surrounded by positive energy. I made sure not to waste a single day and stopped pouring energy into meaningless pursuits.

In 2025, I met many new friends and good partners—excellent individuals with whom I can collaborate and learn.

In 2025, I drew closer to the beauty of Computer Science and AI, rapidly mastering many practical technologies.

In 2025, my parents grew a year older; I am determined to provide them with an incredibly wonderful life in the future.

In 2025, I stopped viewing the world through a limited lens and started seeing problems from the perspective of creating value and changing the world. I have grown to love challenges and risks even more.

In 2025, I prioritized the improvement of real capabilities—growing through practice and creation—rather than **"overfitting"** to useless dogmas.

In 2025, I understood that pain and setbacks are gifts. Without pain, there is no growth; without setbacks, there is no transformation. I hope for more challenges in the future to experience, endure, and accelerate my growth.

In 2025, I worked on many interesting projects with my team, understanding the importance of team building and realizing the true value of teamwork.

In 2025, I figured out how to learn, plan for the future, and cultivate my abilities in the AI era, discerning what has changed and what is no longer useful.

In 2025, I challenged myself by doing many things I previously dared not do, discovering they weren't that scary after all. The current version of me should fear nothing.

In 2025, I started writing a blog and realized the importance of influence.

In 2025, everything I wanted to do has slowly been set in motion. The transformation and the journey have only just begun. Let us wait and see.
